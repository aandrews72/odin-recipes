Scrambeled Eggs Recipe

My typical breakfast

This is my breakfast almost every morning. You can substitute different features of it but here are the ingredients and recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 6 Egglands Best eggs
  • 1 tbsp of unrefiend virgin coconut oil
  • 1/4 of diced onions and bell peppers each
  • A pinch of salt and garlic powder
  • (Optional) A cup of Peet's Coffee

Recipe Instructions

  1. Place pan on stove and set to low
  2. Put 1 tbsp of unrefined virgin coconut oil onto pan
  3. Crack 6 eggs in a bowl and mix
  4. Pour egg yolk onto pan
  5. Dump onions and peppers onto the eggs
  6. Move eggs around pan with spatula as needed
  7. After about 6-7 minutes put the eggs onto a plate and sprinkle salt and garlic powder
  8. Enjoy!
