Smoothie Recipe

My favorite smoothie

This is my breakfast almost every morning. You can substitute different features of it but here are the ingredients and recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp of plain greek yogurt
  • 16 oz of water
  • A cup of blueberries
  • A pinch of salt
  • Two scoops of whey protein

Recipe Instructions

  1. Put the ingredients in the blender beginning with the water
  2. Blend it for 20 seconds
  3. Pour the smoothie into two cups
  4. Enjoy!
