Steak Recipe

My Favorite Meal!

This is my favorite meal! I enjoy my steak medium rare. Here are some ingredients I may use and how I make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of unrefined virgin coconut oil
  • A steak, ideally NY strip
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Salt
  • Brocolli

Recipe

  1. Put a pan on the stove and turn it to medium
  2. Put 1 tbsp of unrefined virgin coconut oil onto the pan
  3. Season the steak with adobo and salt
  4. Put the steak onto the pan
  5. Steam the broccoli in a pot while the steak cooks
  6. Flip the steak every 5 minutes until center is pink
  7. Put the steak and broccoli on the plate
  8. Enjoy!
